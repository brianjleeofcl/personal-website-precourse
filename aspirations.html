Aspirations

First things first,

I am simply thrilled to immerse myself in the fascinating world of web development. Here is a GIF of an exuberant sideline celebration that matches how I feel.

In the last few years I witnessed how technology has drastically changed the ways ideas are expressed and communicated. There are increasingly better means for visualization, publication and coordination—each unbounded by the peculiarities of traditional media. The ever expanding potential for improving how we share the most complex, abstract facts and opinions is exciting to say the least.

Goals

Continuing on this theme, after Galvanize, I would like to find opportunities in areas that can benefit the most from the advances provided by web-based products. More specifically—based on my personal interests—fields related to public policy, which often requires explaining complex concepts to a large audience, continue to hold my attention.
